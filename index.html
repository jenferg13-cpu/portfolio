Habitable Lunar Rover - NASA Intern Design Challenge

Completed: April 2025 Location: NASA Glenn Research Center Role: Team Lead, CAD Designer, Prototype Fabricator

As team lead on this 6-week design challenge, I drove the project from concept to completion; originating the original idea, coordinating team meetings and communications, and owning the full SolidWorks design of a habitable lunar rover built to meet exact design specifications. I also fabricated the physical prototype and played a central role in crafting and delivering the final presentation.

Specs
  • Create a scaled model of a "camper like" habitat for the lunar environment.
  • Allows for EVA performance, lunar sample testing, and 7 days of independent living.
  • Must fit the payload volume, can be pulled by LTV, and docks to a habitat.
  • Included space suits with suit ports (2), beds (2), food & water (7 days), life support, testing equipment, sample storage, power for camper and LTV, and a table.

Awards & Recognitions: Best Project Design, First Place Overall

HVAC Inventory & Maintenance Optimization - GE Appliances

Completed: December 2025 Location: GE Appliances, A Haier Company

Learned AutoCAD and technical drawing interpretation to audit and redesign HVAC inventory and maintenance documentation. Created updated, color-coded technical drawings fact-checked against physical systems, streamlining the maintenance process and improving accuracy. The redesigned system resulted in an 84% reduction in maintenance time and a 74% reduction in related costs.

Specs
  • Update verified physical system data.
  • Create a label system to track refrigerant charges.
  • Follow EHS standards for documentation and labeling.

Awards & Recognitions: Most Impactful Project at Co-Op Report Out Event

Prototype Drop Rig Design - NASA CELERE Project

Completed: May 2025 Location: NASA Glenn Research Center

Designed and built two protoype drop rigs and six controlled test cells using SolidWorks and laser cutting, translating research-based design constraints into physical hardware for testing. Delivered full assembled, ready rigs for experimental trials.

Specs
  • Create a rig that will withstand the impact of a 8 story drop.
  • Contain the viscous oil at the base of the cell.
  • Make the rig portable and easy to replicate.
